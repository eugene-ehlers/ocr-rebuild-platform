# Credit / Trade Credit Decision Service Menu — Structured v1

## 1. Service family purpose

This service family provides credit and trade credit decisioning using financial document intelligence.

It is not an OCR engine itself but uses OCR outputs, enrichment, and analysis to generate actionable credit decisions.

## 2. Service family goals

- Verify document authenticity
- Assess affordability and repayment capacity
- Detect transaction fraud or manipulation
- Generate credit risk score
- Provide best collection / action timing guidance
- Track repayment trends and history
- Generate actionable insights for credit providers

## 3. Delivery channels

### Customer-facing
- Credit approval notifications
- Repayment recommendations
- Payment schedule summaries

### Internal-facing
- Risk scoring dashboards
- Fraud and integrity flags
- Audit and compliance reports

## 4. Service menu structure

Credit / Trade Credit Decision is structured into:
- Core services
- Differentiation services
- Platform expansion
