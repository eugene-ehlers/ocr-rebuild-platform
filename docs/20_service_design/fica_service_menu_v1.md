# FICA Compliance Service Menu — Structured v1

## 1. Service family purpose

FICA Compliance is the service family that turns financial statements into actionable compliance intelligence for KYC/FICA checks.

It is not OCR as a product. OCR is a supplier.
It is a set of customer-facing and internal-facing services that use OCR, extraction, models, rules, and decision logic to deliver compliance decisions.

## 2. Service family goals

This family must support:

- Document authenticity and integrity checks
- Identity and account owner verification
- Transaction compliance checks (anti-money laundering, unusual activity)
- Risk flagging and prioritization
- Detailed traceable explanations for internal audit
- Confidence scoring per step
- Multi-language support where required

## 3. Delivery channels

### Customer-facing
- Confirm KYC/FICA status
- Receive alerts or requests for additional documents

### Internal-facing
- Review flags, risk levels, and anomalies
- Investigate compliance issues
- Support audit and regulatory reporting

## 4. Service menu structure

FICA Compliance is structured into Core, Differentiation, and Platform Expansion services.

# Core FICA Services

## Document Validation Service
- Checks document authenticity, fonts, logos, layout, colors, creation dates
- Flags altered or potentially fraudulent documents

## Identity & Owner Verification Service
- Validates account holder information, names, addresses, and issuer context
- Ensures documents match known records

## Transaction Compliance Service
- Analyses transactions for FICA/KYC compliance
- Detects unusual, suspicious, or high-risk transactions
