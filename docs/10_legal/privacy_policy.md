2. PRIVACY POLICY
1. Overview
1.1 This policy explains how personal information is collected, used, and protected.
1.2 It aligns with POPIA and incorporates GDPR-aligned principles.

2. Information Collected
2.1 Personal information may include:
identity data
financial data
uploaded documents

2.2 Derived data may include:
OCR outputs
financial indicators
risk or behavioural signals

3. Purpose of Processing
3.1 Data is processed for:
document extraction
financial analysis
service delivery
system improvement

4. Lawful Basis
4.1 Processing is based on:
user consent
contractual necessity
legitimate interest (where applicable)

5. Special Personal Information
5.1 Financial and identity data may be considered sensitive.
5.2 Additional safeguards are applied.

6. Data Sharing
6.1 Data may be shared with:
infrastructure providers
analytics services
authorised third parties (with consent)

6.2 No sale of personal data occurs.

7. Data Retention
7.1 Data is retained:
only as long as necessary
according to operational and legal requirements

7.2 Users may request deletion where applicable.

8. Security
8.1 Measures include:
encryption
access controls
monitoring

8.2 No system is completely secure; reasonable safeguards are maintained.

9. Data Subject Rights
9.1 Users may:
access their data
request correction
request deletion
withdraw consent

10. Cross-Border Transfers
10.1 Data may be processed outside South Africa.
10.2 Safeguards ensure adequate protection.

11. Breach Notification
11.1 Users will be notified of material breaches where required.
